Virtual Experience

The Digital Transformation Virtual Experience Program provided me a simulated work experience scenario aimed at exploring and addressing the digital transformation needs of a client, CoffeeCo. Through a series of tasks, I engaged in market research, use case development, impact assessment, data analysis, and project management within a closed environment. The exercise allowed me to integrate knowledge and techniques acquired throughout my study into practical scenarios relevant to digital transformation and business strategy. Completion of these tasks highlights my ability to apply academic knowledge and skills in a real world context.
